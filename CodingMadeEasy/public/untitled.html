    Welcome to the world of Pokemon!


    Your main goal is to complete all 7 tasks. To do this, you must talk with the people on your road, collect information from the boards, fight and catch the wild Pokemon.You will find wild Pokemon in the high grasses. During the fight, you can use your Pokemon's attacks, your luggage and pokeballs in which you can catch them.You can see your current score in the 'Ranking', after you complete one of the seven task. Complete them as soon as possible, and you may find yourself in the first place!To control your character use the 'WSAD' keys and mouse.
